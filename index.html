Main Courses

Lechon

Succulent roasted pork with crispy skin, a centerpiece of every Filipino Christmas table.

Chicken Relleno

Stuffed chicken delicately seasoned and baked to perfection.

Pancit Malabon

A festive noodle dish loaded with seafood and vibrant flavors.

Side Dishes

Puto Bumbong

Purple steamed rice cakes served with butter and grated coconut, a quintessential Christmas delicacy.

Ensaladang Talong

Grilled eggplant salad with tomatoes, onions, and a tangy dressing.

Desserts

Bibingka

Soft rice cake topped with salted egg and cheese, baked in banana leaves for that authentic flavor.

Puto

Steamed rice cakes in various flavors, perfect for pairing with savory dishes or enjoying on their own.

Fruit Salad

A medley of fruits, cream, and condensed milk, a refreshing end to a hearty meal.

Beverages

Tsokolate Eh

Rich and thick Filipino hot chocolate made from tablea or cocoa tablets.

Sago't Gulaman

Sweet and refreshing drink with tapioca pearls and jelly cubes in brown sugar syrup.
