Tsokolate Eh Recipe

Video Tutorial: Tsokolate Eh Recipe Video

Tsokolate Eh is a traditional Filipino hot chocolate drink that's perfect for cold Christmas mornings. Here's how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups water
  • 3-4 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar, or to taste
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Tablea (Filipino cocoa tablets) for a more authentic flavor (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, bring water to a simmer.
  2. Add cocoa powder and stir until dissolved.
  3. Gradually add evaporated milk while stirring constantly.
  4. Season with sugar and a pinch of salt, adjusting to taste.
  5. If using, add vanilla extract for extra flavor.
  6. If using tablea, add them to the saucepan and stir until completely melted.
  7. Simmer for a few more minutes until the tsokolate is hot and well combined.
  8. Pour into mugs and serve hot.
  9. Enjoy your comforting Tsokolate Eh!
