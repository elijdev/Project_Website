Ensaladang Talong Recipe

Ensaladang Talong is a simple yet delicious Filipino eggplant salad. Here's how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium-sized eggplants
  • 3 medium-sized tomatoes, diced
  • 1 medium-sized onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons bagoong (shrimp paste)
  • 1 tablespoon calamansi juice or vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions and/or cilantro for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Grill or broil the eggplants until the skin is charred and the flesh is soft. Let them cool.
  2. Peel off the skin of the eggplants and slice them lengthwise into strips.
  3. Arrange the eggplant slices on a serving plate.
  4. In a bowl, mix together diced tomatoes, sliced onion, bagoong, calamansi juice or vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour the tomato-onion mixture over the eggplant slices.
  6. Garnish with chopped green onions and/or cilantro, if desired.
  7. Serve as a side dish or appetizer with your favorite main course.
  8. Enjoy your refreshing Ensaladang Talong!
